Common Questions
How do I join a queue?
Scan the QR at the entrance or open the business link → enter name → get token instantly.
What does my token like G001 mean?
G = General queue, E = Emergency, F = Follow-up. Number is your position when you joined.
Can I leave and come back?
Check the Safe-to-Leave indicator — green means go, yellow means stay close, red means stay!
How will I know when it's my turn?
Your screen flashes, confetti fires, sound plays, and your phone vibrates. Impossible to miss!
How do I add my business?
Go to register.html → fill details → get your unique queue link and QR code instantly.
How does AI predict wait time?
It tracks how fast people are actually being served and calculates your real ETA — gets smarter over time.
